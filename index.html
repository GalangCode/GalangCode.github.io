About Us

Welcome to Banua Ekspor Mandiri,the pioneer in wood charcoal exports from indonesia renowned for its exceptional quality.We present the finest wood charcoal that ignites the magic of flavors in your grilling experience.From our carefully selected collection,you will find wood charcoal sourced from Indonesia,meticulously processed to ensure unrivaled excellence for your barbecuing needs.

Banua Ekspor Mandiri was formed because of the awarness of young people who see the potential for natural resources around them that have high quality compete in international markets.With the mission of helping many farmers and intoducing their products to the world,increasing the country's foreign exchange,and also advanting countries with international trade.

Why Bukit Borneo Bersama

Premium Quality

Our wood charcoal undergoes rigorous selection, ensuring only the highest-quality pieces grace your grill. Taste the difference in every bite.

Sustainable Sourcing

We are committed to environmentally sustainable practices. Our wood charcoal is sourced from responsibly managed forests, promoting conservation and preserving our natural resources.

Outstanding Performance

Experience the perfect balance of heat and endurance with our wood charcoal. It provides consistent heat distribution, allowing you to achieve optimal cooking results.

Trusted Export Services

With our efficient export processes, we ensure timely delivery of your wood charcoal orders to any destinastion worldwide. Entrust us with your international requirements, and experience professionalism and efficiency.
